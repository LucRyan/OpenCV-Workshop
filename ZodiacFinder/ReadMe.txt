--------------------------------------------------------------
----------        ZodiacFinder Version 1.1.3        ----------
--------------------------------------------------------------

- Preparation:
  Put all the images into the Debug folder.

- Operation procedure:
  1. Enter the year you were born into the command line.
  2. When the Camera open press 'c' to capture your photo, 
     then press 'ESC' to continue. ( You could press 'c' to 
     capture your photo many times as you want, it will save
     the latest image.)
  3. Wait for the application process your image, and enjoy it.
